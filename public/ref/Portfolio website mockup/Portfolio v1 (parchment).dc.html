01 — About

Meet the Barista

I'm a senior at Boston University studying Economics and Computer Science. I got hooked on markets the same way I got hooked on coffee — by being curious about what's actually happening underneath the surface, and refusing to take the easy answer.

These days I split my time between quant research (vol surfaces, backtests, the occasional Monte Carlo rabbit hole) and shipping clean, fast web apps. I care a lot about legible work — code and analysis someone else can read at a glance and trust.

Off the clock you'll find me dialing in a pour-over, reading market history, or rebuilding something that already works just to understand it better.

What's in my cup right now

"Reading Trading and Exchanges by Larry Harris — slowly, with a notebook."

Currently brewing a real-time interest-rate dashboard and learning my way around options market microstructure.
